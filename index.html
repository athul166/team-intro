Projects

The host of projects that the team is engaged in finding solutions for...

Order Routing System-ORS
This is to mimic real world Order Management System using microservices and to decide the least routing algorithm based on distance of the Distribution centre from the customer.

Conversational Commerce Bot – Melissa
This is a personal assistant sitting behind Google Home which will help in performing online transactions in a retail website using conversations.

Do It Yourself - Add To Cart
Bloggers can add details about the ingredients/supplies needed to their Do It Yourself videos while they upload online. Customer who view this will have a button on the video clicking on which will show them a portal to add the things to their choice of online shopping portal and checkout.

WMG Automation:
This is to automate the internal resource search by a Wipro WMG Personnel using conversational capability Search based on
1) Location
2) Skill set
3) Band

Self Service BBYD Partner Store Front:
This is a proposal to simplify a Partner registration into Best Buy - B2B Portal and to enable him a speedy smooth sail

Secure Supply Chain:
The data stored for every ingredient that goes into a Food product can be used by the customer in deciding their choice of product. This is demonstrated through blockchain and a front end.

1) 1-Click Travel Booking:
This app uses personality & social status analysis and enables the user with a 1-click travel booking for Flight, Hotel and Rental Car. He can manage his current bookings and also see suggested travel itineraries.
2) Crew Dashboard:
Flight crew can use this application in-flight to serve the passengers. Notifications will be pushed to their dashboard through the data analytics.
3) Persona Analytics:
This gives a bird's eye view of a passenger's personal, social data that the Hospitality and Travel domain partners can use to engage with the customers

Rule based Inventory Allocation POC:
Purchase Order(PO) allocation to Processing Center(PC) based on flexible rules engine. Once a PO is placed by TJX to a supplier, this rules engine will identify the carrier, mode of delivery (exp/normal), and then apply a few rules based on business needs to deliver them to a PC. ,
1) Happy path - Entire PO is sent to one PC
2) All rules fail and no delivery scheduled
3) Manual allocation overriding rules.

Diageo Sales Analytic:
This demonstrates the sales performance of Diageo over a time period and through various partner channels. This way Diageo can look at their sales performance in the past and decide on a projection for forthcoming quarters.

Rental Car Booking using Loyalty Points across Partners - Chat Assistant:
This is to demonstrate the use of loyalty points across different partners viz, Flight, Hotel and Rental Cars which will be networked through blockchain to book an itinerary with just one partner.
